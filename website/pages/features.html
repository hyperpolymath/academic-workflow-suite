AI-Assisted Feedback Generation

Intelligent, personalized feedback at scale

Generate high-quality, constructive feedback for student assignments using advanced AI technology. Our system analyzes submissions against your custom rubrics and generates personalized, actionable feedback that helps students improve.

Key Capabilities

  • Contextual Understanding: AI analyzes the full context of student work, identifying strengths and areas for improvement based on learning objectives.
  • Rubric-Based Scoring: Automatically score assignments against your custom rubrics with detailed justifications for each criterion.
  • Personalization: Feedback adapts to individual student needs, considering their academic level and previous performance.
  • Multi-Format Support: Process essays, reports, code submissions, presentations, and more with format-specific analysis.
  • Tone Control: Customize the tone of feedback from encouraging to constructively critical based on your teaching style.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: All AI-generated feedback is reviewed and can be edited before being shared with students.

Time Savings

Educators report saving 50-70% of marking time while maintaining or improving feedback quality. That's hours back in your week for research, teaching prep, or personal time.

Privacy-First Design

Your data, your control, always

We built Academic Workflow Suite with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. All student data processing happens locally on your machine, with optional AI isolation that ensures sensitive information never leaves your control.

Privacy Features

  • Local Processing: All data processing happens on your computer. No student information is sent to external servers.
  • AI Isolation Mode: Optional sandboxed environment that strips personally identifiable information before AI processing.
  • Encrypted Storage: All local data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
  • Zero Telemetry: We don't track your usage or collect analytics without explicit opt-in consent.
  • Offline Capable: Core features work completely offline. No internet connection required.
  • Audit Logs: Complete transparency with detailed logs of all data access and processing.

University-Approved

Academic Workflow Suite meets the strict privacy requirements of leading universities worldwide. See our security documentation for detailed compliance information.

Intelligent Batch Processing

Handle hundreds of submissions efficiently

Process multiple assignments simultaneously while maintaining consistency and quality. Our batch processing engine intelligently distributes work and ensures uniform feedback standards.

Batch Features

  • Parallel Processing: Process multiple submissions simultaneously, optimized for your hardware.
  • Progress Tracking: Real-time progress updates with estimated completion times.
  • Consistency Checks: Automated detection of inconsistent scoring across submissions.
  • Priority Queuing: Mark urgent assignments first or process by submission date.
  • Resume Capability: Pause and resume batch jobs without losing progress.
  • Quality Control: Review samples from batches before releasing all feedback.

Command Line Interface

# Process all submissions for an assignment
aws-cli batch-mark --assignment "Essay 2023" --rubric "essay-rubric.yaml"

# Process with custom parallelism
aws-cli batch-mark --assignment "Lab Report 5" --workers 4

# Review mode: generate feedback but don't publish
aws-cli batch-mark --assignment "Final Project" --review-only

Custom Rubrics & Templates

Define your grading criteria, maintain consistency

Create, save, and reuse custom marking rubrics tailored to your course requirements. Ensure consistent grading standards across assignments, semesters, and teaching teams.

Rubric Features

  • Flexible Structure: Define criteria, sub-criteria, and weighted scoring schemes.
  • Descriptive Levels: Set clear performance level descriptions for each criterion.
  • Template Library: Start from pre-built templates for common assignment types.
  • Version Control: Track rubric changes over time and maintain historical records.
  • Collaborative Editing: Share rubrics with colleagues and co-develop standards.
  • Import/Export: Use YAML, JSON, or Excel formats for easy editing and sharing.

Example Rubric (YAML)

name: "Research Essay Rubric"
total_points: 100
criteria:
  - name: "Argument & Analysis"
    weight: 40
    levels:
      - score: 4
        description: "Exceptionally clear thesis with sophisticated analysis"
      - score: 3
        description: "Clear thesis with good supporting analysis"
      - score: 2
        description: "Basic thesis with limited analysis"
      - score: 1
        description: "Unclear or missing thesis"

  - name: "Research & Sources"
    weight: 30
    levels:
      - score: 4
        description: "Excellent use of scholarly sources"
      - score: 3
        description: "Good use of relevant sources"
      # ... more levels
