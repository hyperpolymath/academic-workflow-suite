How It Works

Get started in minutes, not hours

1

Install & Configure

Download the Academic Workflow Suite and run the quick setup wizard. Configure your preferences and integrate with your existing tools.

2

Import Your Work

Import assignments from your LMS or upload directly. The system automatically organizes and categorizes your content.

3

Review & Customize

AI generates initial feedback based on your rubric. Review, edit, and personalize the feedback to match your teaching style.

4

Export & Share

Export feedback directly to your LMS or download as PDF. Students receive constructive, personalized guidance instantly.
