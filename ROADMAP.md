# Academic Workflow Suite Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned features and improvements for the Academic Workflow Suite. This is a living document and will be updated as priorities change and new ideas emerge.

**Last Updated**: 2025-11-22

## Vision

To become the comprehensive toolkit that empowers researchers, students, and academics to automate and streamline their workflows, allowing them to focus on what matters most: their research and scholarship.

## Guiding Principles

1. **User-Centric**: Design with academic users in mind
2. **Interoperable**: Integrate seamlessly with existing academic tools
3. **Reliable**: Maintain high quality and stability
4. **Extensible**: Enable community plugins and extensions
5. **Open**: Maintain transparency and open-source ethos

## Release Timeline

```
2025 Q1-Q2: Foundation & Core Features (v0.1 - v1.0)
2025 Q3-Q4: Integration & Enhancement (v1.1 - v1.5)
2026 H1: Advanced Features (v2.0)
2026 H2+: Community-Driven Development
```

## Versioning Strategy

We follow [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/):

- **MAJOR** (X.0.0): Breaking changes
- **MINOR** (x.Y.0): New features, backwards-compatible
- **PATCH** (x.y.Z): Bug fixes, backwards-compatible

### Release Cadence

- **Patch releases**: As needed (bug fixes)
- **Minor releases**: Every 2-3 months
- **Major releases**: Annually or when breaking changes accumulated
